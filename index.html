Xingdi Wang ✵ 王星迪

　　°Wang Xingdi (2000) graduated from the Electronic Musical Instrument Engineering program at Xinghai Conservatory of Music and is currently enrolled in the Electronic Music Department at the Giuseppe Verdi Conservatory of Music in Milan.

Her creative pursuits encompass a variety of genres, including sound interactive installations, synthesizer software design, acousmatic music, and 3D visual projects.

Her achievements include winning the Bronze Award in the International Electronic Music Interactive Media category at the Danny Awards, as well as an Excellence Award in Applied Music, third prize in the NCDA Future Designers Competition for Digital Music in Guangdong province and received an Outstanding Work Award at the National Conference on Sound and Music Technology (CMST).
